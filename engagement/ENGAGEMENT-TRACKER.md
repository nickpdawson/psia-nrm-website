## Connection tools (instructor → client) — toward Jan 2027

Simple, dignified tools for instructors to share their passion with clients/parents — **connection and awareness, never a money ask**. The instructor's whole job is one line: *"would you mind if I shared this story with my organization?"* — then the client pulls and the site does the rest. Doubles as a content-sourcing engine. Full concept: `connection-tools-concept.md`. Target: live and piloted before the Our Mountain launch.
