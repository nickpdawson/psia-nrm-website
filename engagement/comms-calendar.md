# Member Communications Calendar (proposed)

**Source:** Proposed at the board meeting (June 2026). **Flexible** — a skeleton to flex against, not a contract.
**What it is:** the cadence/rhythm of member communications (newsletter-style). Our content (video series + member stories) is the *substance* that fills these slots; the site is where every send points.

---

## The proposed cadence

| Month | #/yr | When | Topic |
|-------|------|------|-------|
| January | 2 | 1st & 3rd week | — |
| February | 2 | 1st & last week | *Stoke* |
| March | 1 | 2nd week | Sun protection? · **BOD Election** |
| April | 1 | 1st week | End-of-season gear care? · **BOD Election** |
| May | 1 | 1st week | Post-season conclusions? |
| June | 1 | 1st week | Upcoming dues — where does that money go? · *Stoke* |
| July | 1 | 1st week | Did you pay your dues? |
| August | 1 | 1st week | Calendar is getting made — any suggestions? |
| September | 1 | 1st week | How was the summer? Pre-season training · *Stoke* |
| October | 1 | 1st week | Event sign-up, planning your season |
| November | 2 | 1st & 3rd week | Upcoming clinics, live calendar, Fall Fest · *Stoke* |
| December | 1 | 1st week | Here we go! |

*"Stoke" = the inspirational/community beat (Feb, June, Sept, Nov). These are exactly where emotional, member-story content shines.*

---

## How our content fills it

| Month | Comms slot | What we drop in |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Aug** | "Calendar getting made / suggestions" | **Announce the NEW SITE launch** (~Aug 3). The single biggest comms moment of the year — make this the reveal. |
| **Sept** | *Stoke* · pre-season training | **Katie's strength & cross-training series** is purpose-built for this slot. |
| **Oct** | Event sign-up, planning | **Jill's events/clinics/exams** content; **Brenna's** analysis to get people training. |
| **Nov** | *Stoke* · clinics / Fall Fest | More Jill + a **"Why I Teach"** story to carry the stoke. |
| **Dec** | "Here we go!" | A **"Why I Teach"** story to open the season on emotion. |
| **Jan (×2)** | 1st & 3rd week | **Our Mountain soft launch lands here** — two sends in January is a ready-made launch vehicle into a warmed audience. |
| **Feb / June** | *Stoke* | Rotate in the strongest **member stories** + Brenna coaching clips. |

---

## Notes & flags

- **The "Stoke" beats are the Connection engine in disguise.** Feb / June / Sept / Nov already call for inspirational content — that's where the "Why I Teach" library does its work. The calendar and the Connection strategy are the same plan.
- **January's double send is a gift for Our Mountain.** The campaign soft-launch (Phase 3) slots naturally into the Jan cadence — no need to invent a new channel.
- **June "where does that money go?"** is a chance to build value/awareness *before* any 2027 ask — transparency, not solicitation. Aligns with the Connection-not-fundraising principle.
- **Aug = launch megaphone.** Sequence the site go-live so the August comms can be the unveiling.
- **Cross-channel:** this is the newsletter rhythm; video content lives on the site + socials and gets *featured* in these sends. Every send routes back to the hub.

> Treat as flexible. The value is the rhythm + the content-to-slot mapping, not the exact weeks.
